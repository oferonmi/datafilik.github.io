Gutenkraft, which literally means 'good strength or good force', is a non-profit technology research and development institute with a focus on problems in the developing world. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems and drive growth by the adoption of open source and public domain technologies.

Mission:

To explore public domain technologies with a focus on improving and adapting them in solutions to identified problems in the developing world.

Vision:

To develop world class industry solutions to drive technology growth in the developing world.
