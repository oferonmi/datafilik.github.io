What is Gutenkraft?

Gutenkraft, which literally means 'good strength or good force', is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on tackling problems in developing nations using technology. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems and reduce perpetual reliance on foreign aid. The aim is to drive growth and advancement for sustainable improvement of living standards by innovating on and adapting technologies to develop and deploy the necessary building blocks of key growth infrastructures. This is codified in our mission and vision.

Our Mission:

To adapt public domain technologies and innovate towards establishing sustainable technology infrastructures in developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable and sustainable world class technology infrastructure solutions on a large scale.
