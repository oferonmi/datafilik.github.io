What is Gutenkraft?

Gutenkraft, which literally means 'good strength or good force', is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on tackling problems in developing countries using techology. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems (instead of reliance on Aid). The aim is to drive growth and advancement for sustainable improvement of living standards.

Our Mission:

To innovate, improve and adapt public domain technologies to solve problems in the developing world.

Our Vision:

To build affordable and sustainable world class technology solutions.
