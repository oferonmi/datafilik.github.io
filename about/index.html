What is Gutenkraft?

Gutenkraft is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on tackling problems in developing nations using technology. The name is a portmanteau word for 'good strength or good force'. We will work to create and implement a technology driven blue-print that the developing world could use to handle her problems and reduce perpetual reliance on foreign aid. The aim is to drive growth and advancement for sustainable improvement of living standards by innovating on and adapting technologies to develop and deploy the necessary building blocks of key growth infrastructures. This is codified in our mission and vision.

Our Mission:

To adapt public domain technologies and innovate towards establishing sustainable technology infrastructures and solutions in developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable world class technology solutions on a large scale.
