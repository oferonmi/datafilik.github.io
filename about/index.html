Gutenkraft is a non-profit focused on researching and developing open source based technologies for the developing world. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems and drive growth by the adoption of open source technologies.

Gutenkraft's Mission:

To explore open source technology projects, methodologies, tools and processes with a focus on improving and adapting them for driving development of applications to identified problems in the developing world.

Gutenkraft's Vision:

To develop world class industry solutions to drive technology growth in the developing world.
