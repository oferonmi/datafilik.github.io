Gutenkraft, which literally means 'good strength or good force', is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) institute with a focus on problems in the developing world. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems and drive growth by the adoption of open source and public domain technologies.

Mission:

To improve and adapt public domain technologies to solve identified problems in the developing world.

Vision:

To develop world class industry technology solutions to drive growth in the developing world.
