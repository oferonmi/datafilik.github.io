What is Gutenkraft?

Gutenkraft, which literally means 'good strength or good force', is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on tackling problems in developing countries using techology. We hope to create a blue-print of how the developing world could handle her problems (instead of reliance on Aid). The aim is to drive growth and advancement by the adoption of and improvement on open source and public domain technologies.

Mission:

To improve and adapt public domain technologies to solve problems in the developing world.

Vision:

To build world class technology industry solutions to drive growth in the developing world.
