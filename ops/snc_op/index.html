Signal and Connectivity (S&C) Ops

This op's key focus is on investigating and developing novel techniques for acquisition, fusion, processing, transmission and reception of telemetric and communcation signals in smart network systems (SNS) like smart grids, smart cities, Industry 4.0 etc. The current focus on enabling high frequency bands such as Millimetre wave (mmWave) & Tetrahertz (THz) wave band for broadband wireless terrestial networks.

Futhermore, it will explore frontiers in the development and deployment of hybrid network architectures. Hybrid networks are modular in nature and can operate in a centralized as well as a descentralized mode. Blockchains and other peer-to-peer(P2P) systems and protocols already provide certain foundations that can be built upon for creating such systems.

Smart hybrid networks could be crucial to creating robust machine networks that take advantage of the resilence of decentralized systems as well as the efficiency of centralized systems.
