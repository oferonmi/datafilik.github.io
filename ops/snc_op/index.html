Signal and Connectivity (S&C) Ops

This operational focus area is concerned with developing novel processing technique for aquired and/or fused signals from sensors in communication and wireless sensor networks.

Also, this op focuses on exploring frontiers in the development and deployment of hybrid architectures for machine networks. Hybrid architecture are modular in nature and thus can operate in centralized as well as a descentralized mode. Blockchains and other peer-to-peer systems already provide certain foundations that can be built upon for creating decentralized systems.

We hybrid network allows for creating robust machine networks that take advantage of the resilence of decentralized systems as well as the efficiency of centralized systems.
