Signal and Connectivity (S&C) Ops

This op's key focus is on investigating and developing novel techniques for acquisition, fusion, processing, transmission and reception of telemetric and communcation signals in smart systems or networks (Like smart grids, smart cities, etc.).

Futhermore, it will explore frontiers in the development and deployment of smart hybrid machine network architectures. Hybrid architecture are modular in nature can operate in a centralized as well as a descentralized mode. Blockchains and other peer-to-peer systems already provide certain foundations that can be built upon for creating such systems.

Smart hybrid networks could be crucial to creating robust machine networks that take advantage of the resilence of decentralized systems as well as the efficiency of centralized systems.
