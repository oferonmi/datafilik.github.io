Computer aided design and manufacturing (CAD&M) Ops

The focus of this op is to muster and/or develop expertize in Open source Electronic design automation (EDA) and 2D/3D CAD softwares for fabless semiconductor development and design of precision engineered systems respectively. An initial focus will be on EDA tools such as,

  • ▸ KiCAD
  • ▸ LibrePCB

For CAD&M initial tool of focus include,

  • ▸ FreeCAD

This would be handy for quickly developing system prototypes and providing workable blueprints or schematics, core IP and etching masks for semicondutor and other hardware production.
