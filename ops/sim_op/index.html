Simulation Ops

The focus of Sim ops is to explore and adopt scientific models and computer-generated-imagery (CGI) to develope a visualy compelling and easy-to-use Genral purpose physics engine.

The intent is to create a tool for conducting validated studies in a virtual enviroment for research purpose as well as to inform technology product development. Also, an alternate version that has a game-play element to it is intended to facilitate game-based-learning (GBL) to help induce undestanding of concepts and ideas key to development of technology products.
