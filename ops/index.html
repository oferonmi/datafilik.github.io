Ops refers to operational focus areas that are considered essential to projects that Gutenkraft undertakes. Each op consists of a grouping of talents/skills specfic to the op and would serve as in-house sounding-board that players in each project can consult. The ops list is not fixed and may be updated from time to time depending on org wide focus as well as development of new expertize. These includes,

  • Embedded Ops
  • Signal & connectivity (S&C) Ops
  • Computer aided design and manufacturing (CAD&M) Ops
  • Simulation Ops
  • Algorithm Ops
  • Data Ops
  • Digital Art Ops

The expertize or skills developed or mustered in each ops unit, would occassionaly work in concert towards developing and implementing solutions for specific problem of focus in a Gutenkraft project.
