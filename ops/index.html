Ops refers to operational focus areas at Gutenkraft. The ops list would be updated from time to time depending on org wide focus as well as development of new expertize. These includes,

  • CAD/M Ops
  • Simulation Ops
  • Analytics Ops
  • Embedded Ops
  • Automation Ops
  • Signal Ops
  • Network Ops
  • Digital Art Ops

The expertize or skills developed or mustered in each ops unit, would occassionaly work in concert towards developing and implementing solutions for specific problem of focus in a Gutenkraft project.
