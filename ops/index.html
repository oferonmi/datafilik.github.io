Ops refers to operational focus areas that are considered as essential to projects that Gutenkraft undertakes. Each op consists of a group of talents specfic to the op and would serve as in-house sounding-board that players in each project can consult. The ops list would be updated from time to time depending on org wide focus as well as development of new expertize. These includes,

  • CAD/M Ops
  • Simulation Ops
  • Embedded & Fabless (E&F) Ops
  • connectivity Ops
  • Signal Ops
  • Algorithm Ops
  • Data Ops
  • Digital Art Ops

The expertize or skills developed or mustered in each ops unit, would occassionaly work in concert towards developing and implementing solutions for specific problem of focus in a Gutenkraft project.
