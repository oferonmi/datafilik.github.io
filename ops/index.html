Ops refers to operation focus areas at Gutenkraft. These includes,

  • Dev Ops
  • CAD/M Ops
  • Simulation Ops
  • Embedded Ops
  • Signal Ops
  • Network Ops
  • Digital Art Ops

Expertize or skills developed in some or all of the focus units would work in concert towards developing and implementing solutions for specific problem area.
