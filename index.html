Welcome to Gutenkraft


Who We Are.

Driven by the desire to make foreign aid effective in driving development and enabling self-sufficiency of developing nations, Gutenkraft Labs is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on crafting and deploying building blocks of growth infrastructures, to drive development and improve quality of life in developing nations.


The Problem.

According to the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), this is the level of foreign aid as of year 2020 (The year of COVID-19 pandemic) was $161.2 billion. This was an increase of about 3.5% from 2019 levels and the highest level yet. Inspite of this increase in generosity, questions continues to be asked about the effectiveness of foreign aid because of the persistence of problems into which much fund had been sunk in the past. The challenge of ensuring foreign aid effectiveness is in large part due to its use mosty for implementing band-aid sort of solutions, which are often unsustainable quick fixes .


Our Position.

At Gutenkraft, we believe the lack of necessary growth infrastructures (both at individual and communal levels), is a key factor responsible for driving these unsustainable quick fix approaches to foreign aid deployment.


Our Priority.

Our Mission:

To adapt public domain technologies and innovate towards establishing sustainable technology infrastructures in developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable and sustainable world class technology infrastructure solutions on a large scale.
