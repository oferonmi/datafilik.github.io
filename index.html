Why Gutenkraft?


$161 billion

According to the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), this is the level of foreign aid as of year 2020 (The year of COVID-19 pandemic). It is an increase of about 3.5% from 2019 levels and the highest level yet.

This challenge of ensuring foreign aid effectiveness is in large part due to its use mosty for implementing band-aid sort of solutions, which are quick temporary fix that are often unsustainable.

At Gutenkraft, we believe the lack of approprate technological and other base infrastures (both at individual and communal levels) is a key reason why these unsustainable quick fix approaches to foreign aid, are common and thus ineffective.
Driven by the desire to make foreign aid effective as well as a tool to enable self-sufficiency of developing nations, Gutenkraft Labs is set-up as a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused exclusively on designing, developing and deploying building blocks of necessary technological infrastructures needed to drive growth and improve quality of life in developing nations. This focus is codified in Gutenkraft's mission and vision.

Gutenkraft's Mission:

To innovate, improve and adapt public domain technologies to solve problems the developing nations.

Gutenkraft's Vision:

To build affordable and sustainable world class technology solutions in large scale.
