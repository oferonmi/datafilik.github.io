Welcome to Gutenkraft

Gutenkraft is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on crafting and deploying sustainable technology infrastructures, to drive development and improve quality of life in developing nations.
Her focus is on rethinking technology infrastructures for developing nations to reduce the barrier to large scale sustainable deployment.


The Problem.

Technology infrastructures can be unweildy, difficult to deploy and maintain. This implies that with the current approach, great capital cost is involved for the entire life cycle of the infrastructure hardware. Given the high deployment and maintenance cost, they are often consigned as oppotunity cost to attending to other easy to solve national needs in developing nations.

What We Think.

At Gutenkraft, we believe the lack of necessary technology facilities in developing and under-developed nations, makes them unable to attract and effectively utilize capital (from foreign and local investment as well as foreign aid) to drive sustainable development and improved quality of life. We think key technology infrastructures need a pragmatic rethink that considers the reality on ground in developing and under-developed nations.

Our Priority.

Our Mission:

To adapt public domain technologies and innovate towards establishing sustainable technology infrastructures and solutions in developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable world class technology solutions on a large scale.
