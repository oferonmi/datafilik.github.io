Welcome to Gutenkraft

Gutenkraft is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit, dedicated to rethinking the development and deployment of technology infrastructures for developing nations, to ensure cost-effectiveness, sustainablity and scalability.


The Problem.

Technology infrastructures can be unweildy, difficult to deploy and maintain. This implies that with the current approach, great capital cost is involved for the entire life cycle of the infrastructure hardware. Given the high deployment and maintenance cost, they become the opportunity cost of attending to other easy-to-solve (often short term impact) national needs in developing nations.


What We Think.

At Gutenkraft, we believe the lack of necessary technological facilities in developing and under-developed nations, makes them unable to attract and effectively utilize capital (from foreign/local investment as well as foreign aid), needed to drive sustainable development and improved quality of life. We think key technology infrastructures need a pragmatic rethink that considers the reality on ground in developing and under-developed nations.


Our Priority.

Our Mission:

To adapt public domain technologies and innovate towards establishing sustainable technology infrastructures and solutions in developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable world class technology solutions on a large scale.
